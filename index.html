Easy Chicken Karahi Recipe

Ingredients
  • 1 whole skinless chicken, cut into curry size pieces
  • 1 large onion, chopped
  • Vegetable oil for cooking
  • 1 tbsp minced or grated garlic
  • Salt to taste
  • 1 tsp black pepper
  • 1 tsp red chilli powder
  • 1 tsp cumin seeds
  • 1 tsp coriander powder
  • 1/4 tsp turmeric powder
  • 3 tomatoes, chopped
  • 2-3 small green chillies
  • 1/2 cup chopped fresh cilantro
  • 1 inch ginger cut julienne style
  • Squeeze of fresh lemon juice (optional)
Did you know

The term desi food refers to food from India, Pakistan or Bangladesh.

Method
  1. Heat oil in pan and saute onion till brown. Be careful not to burn it!
  2. Stir in the minced garlic and saute for a few minutes before adding in the chicken.
  3. As soon as you add the chicken, add in a squeeze of fresh lemon juice. This is entirely optional, but will give you a moist and juicy chicken.
  4. Mix well and cook on medium heat till chicken starts to brown. This should only take a few minutes.
  5. Sprinkle in all your spices, mix well, and cook on medium heat till oil starts to separate from gravy. If at any point you feel that the chicken is sticking to the pan, add splashes of water.
  6. Add in the chopped tomatoes, ginger and green chillies. Mix well and continue coking till tomatoes soften.
  7. Reduce heat and sprinkle in the fresh cilantro, cook till liquid has reduced and oil separates from gravy.
  8. Serve hot with naan or rice!
